About CASA

The Computer Applications Student Association (CASA) is a non-profit socio-cultural meant for the students of Computer Applications, The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda. Computer Applications is the home for hundreds of Computer Application Programme students. CASA represents each and every one of them and is committed to showcasing and highlighting their role in the programme, we also draw a flux of technical as well as non-technical acumen through our various programs. The purpose of the CASA is to provide an opportunity for all Computer Application Programme students through its varied activities, to interact and give exposure to best creative talents among the students.

Mission

To foster an environment that brings together students of Computer Applications, The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda and act as medium to inculcate, strengthen & enhance their technical and non-technical skills.

Vision

1) We strive to enrich the lives of alumni and friends by helping them establish lifelong, meaningful and valued relationships with Computer Applications and with each other.
2) We strive to showcase the presence of Computer Application Programmes and its students globally.

Our Composition

The CASA represents the 440 Computer Application Programme students enrolled every year at BCA and MScST courses at the Computer Applications, The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda, including all the alumni Computer Application Programme students who are always keen to be the guiding force for the upliftment of the Computer Applications.

Functioning and Events

The day-to-day functioning of the association is managed by a team of presidents,heads,executives,associates and volunteers. Our activities range from celebrating events such as Freshers and Farewell to organising Annual Tour and celebrations like Uttrayan,Teacher's day etc... along with Ice Breaking Session for the freshmen. We organise an IT fest - Cyberia during the later months of the year and a Sports Day during the earlier part of the year.
